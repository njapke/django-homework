How To add a TODO

To add a TODO, simply navigate to Example list and click and a form will appear. Use this form, and the onscreen instructions to create your TODO.

How to edit a TODO

  1. Navigate to the Example list.
  2. Choose the TODO you want to edit and click the button.
  3. A form will appear, prompting you to enter new entry values. Simply follow the instructions ODER:
  4. The title, progress and deadline fields will turn into forms, into which you'll be able to enter the updated information. Empty forms will not be persisted.
  5. If you change your mind, just click the Cancel button
